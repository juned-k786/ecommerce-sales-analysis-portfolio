# Insights Summary

## Objective

The goal of this analysis is to understand sales performance, category contribution, regional performance, and profitability trends for a sample e-commerce business.

## Main Results

### 1. Overall Performance

- Total sales: `460,000`
- Total profit: `78,900`
- Overall profit margin: approximately `17.15%`

### 2. Category Performance

- Electronics generated the highest sales at `289,000`
- Furniture generated `108,000` in sales
- Office Supplies generated `63,000` in sales
- Electronics also delivered the highest profit, making it the strongest category overall

### 3. Regional Performance

- North region produced the highest revenue at `157,000`
- West region followed with `134,000`
- East and South showed lower sales performance compared with North and West

### 4. Monthly Trend

- January sales were `125,000`, the highest month in the dataset
- June followed closely with `109,000`
- February and April had noticeably lower performance

### 5. Discount and Profitability Insight

- Furniture shows weaker profitability relative to its sales
- Higher discounts in Furniture appear to reduce profit efficiency
- Electronics remains the most profitable category even when discounts are present

### 6. Payment Behavior

- Card payments account for the highest share of revenue
- UPI is the second-largest payment method by sales value
- COD contributes the least revenue in this sample

## Recommendations

1. Increase promotion of high-performing Electronics products.
2. Review discount strategy for Furniture to protect margins.
3. Investigate low-performing months to understand demand fluctuations.
4. Explore stronger sales campaigns in East and South regions.
5. Build a dashboard to track category, region, and monthly KPIs regularly.
