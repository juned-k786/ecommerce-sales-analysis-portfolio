# E-Commerce Sales Analysis Portfolio Project

## Project Overview

This project analyzes e-commerce order performance across regions, product categories, and months to identify revenue trends, profitability drivers, and improvement opportunities.

It is designed as a beginner-friendly but professional-looking Data Analyst portfolio project using spreadsheet-style data and SQL analysis.

## Business Problem

An e-commerce business wants to understand:


- Which product categories generate the most sales
- Which regions perform best and worst
- How monthly sales are changing over time
- Which areas may need pricing or discount improvements

## Dataset

The dataset contains sample order-level sales data with:

- Order date
- Region and city
- Product category
- Sales value
- Quantity
- Discount percentage
- Profit
- Payment method

File:

- `data/orders.csv`

## Project Questions

1. What is the total revenue and profit?
2. Which category generates the highest sales?
3. Which region performs best?
4. Which month had the highest sales?
5. How do discounts affect profitability?
6. Which payment methods are used most often by sales value?

## Key Findings

- Total sales in the dataset are `460,000`
- Total profit is `78,900`
- Electronics is the top-selling category with `289,000` in sales
- North region leads revenue with `157,000`
- January and June are the strongest months
- Furniture has lower profit efficiency compared with Electronics

## Business Recommendations

- Increase focus on high-performing Electronics products
- Review discount strategy for Furniture, where profit is relatively weak
- Investigate why East and South regions trail behind North and West
- Build a simple dashboard in Excel or Power BI as the next project step
